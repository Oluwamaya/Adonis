What We Offer

ADONIS Football Academy is a residential football academy based at Liberty stadium in Ibadan, providing Football and Development Camps for aspiring footballers. The Academy benefits from top-class football facilities, including top-quality grass and 3G training pitches, analysis centre and state of the art gym with the aim to deliver intensive coaching sessions designed to improve and develop every individual’s game.

To identify, train, support and expose grassroot football talents to the modern game of Football through a structured process of balanced and standard technical, tactical, physical, and psychological training program with the objective of transforming raw grassroot Football talents into Football Stars on the world stage in the nearest future.
